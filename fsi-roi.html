Privacy & Data Use Disclosure

Effective Date: February 2026 · Quantumleap Insights LLC

What We Collect

When you complete the FSI AI ROI Assessment, we collect the contact information you provide (name, work email, company, job title, and company size) along with your assessment inputs (FSI sub-vertical, AI maturity stage, use cases selected, investment parameters, and deployment model choices). We also collect the timestamp of your submission and your stated area of consulting interest.

How We Use Your Information

Primary use: To follow up with you regarding your assessment results, to provide additional analysis, and to discuss whether Quantumleap Insights' advisory services are a fit for your organization's AI investment needs.

Service improvement: Aggregated, anonymized assessment data is used to continuously improve the accuracy of our ROI framework, calibrate industry benchmarks, and develop new use case models. Individual responses are never published or shared in identifiable form.

Thought leadership: Anonymized, aggregate trends across assessments (e.g., "X% of retail banks are at the Piloting stage") may be referenced in Quantumleap Insights publications or client presentations.

What We Do Not Do

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We do not add you to mass marketing lists without your explicit consent. We do not use your data for any purpose unrelated to AI strategy advisory services.

Data Retention

Contact and assessment data is retained for up to 36 months from the date of submission, or until you request deletion. After this period, data is either deleted or fully anonymized. You may request deletion at any time by emailing insights@quantumleap.llc.

Security

Assessment submissions are transmitted via encrypted HTTPS and stored in Netlify's secure form infrastructure. Access is restricted to authorized Quantumleap Insights personnel only.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at insights@quantumleap.llc. If you are located in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
